Our Expertise

If you are a company that is interested in skin care products, it is time to get yourself familiar with LED light therapy. The increasing prevalence of dermatological diseases coupled with the rising demand for non-invasive treatments will augment industry growth for LED Light therapy. The global light therapy market is forecasted to grow rapidly and surpass USD 1 billion by 2025, according to Global Market Insights Inc.

We are currently active in the Beauty, Sport and Medical industries.

Anti-aging

Red LED Light stimulates collagen and elastin production. This results in the reduction of fine lines and wrinkles while rejuvenating the skin.

Anti-acne

Blue LED Light has anti-microbial properties that help clear the skin from acne. It also reduces oil gland activity preventing and energizes it for a healthy glow.
Hair growth

Red LED Light has a stimulating effect on dermal papilla cells that regulate hair cycling and growth. This results in increased hair count, density, thickness, and strength.

Pain relief

Infra-Red helps cells regenerate or repair themselves and improves the circulation of oxygen-rich blood. This accelerates deep tissue healing while giving a sense of relaxation.

Muscle recovery

Light therapy promotes efficient ATP production and reduces post-workout inflammation, aiding with muscle recovery after a difficult training session.

Oral care

Red LED Light and Blue LED Light have continually found to prevent gum disease, relieve pain, help whiten teeth, and combat oral microbes that cause bad breath.
